Our Story

Founded in the heart of a dust-filled garage where the smell of sawdust met the hum of tube amplifiers, Hippy Chip’s Guitar LLC was born from a simple obsession: the pursuit of the perfect resonance.

What started as a hobby of rescuing "pawn shop prizes" and forgotten relics turned into a mission to bridge the gap between vintage soul and modern technical precision. At Hippy Chip's, we don't just see a guitar as a collection of wood and wire—we see it as a living, breathing extension of the artist.

Our philosophy is simple: Respect the wood, master the tech. Whether we are performing surgical-grade PCB repairs on vintage pedals, carving a custom neck that feels like home, or deep-diving into the electronics of a modern masterpiece, we treat every instrument with the reverence it deserves.

The "Hippy" in our name represents the creative, free-spirited art of luthiery—the belief that every guitar has its own unique voice. The "Chip" represents the micro-precision of the 21st century—the technical expertise required to ensure every circuit is clean and every fret is perfect.

We aren't just a shop; we are a community of builders, players, and gear-heads. From the first solder joint to the final setup, we’re here to make sure your sound is as legendary as your spirit.
